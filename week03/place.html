About Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa, known for its vibrant culture, booming film industry (Nollywood), and rich diversity of over 250 ethnic groups. From the bustling streets of Lagos to the historic city of Kano, Nigeria offers a blend of modern energy and deep tradition.
